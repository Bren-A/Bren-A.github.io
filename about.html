About

My name is Brendan and I am a programmer.

I graduated from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) with a Bachelors in Linguistics and Computer Science in 2019. I love learning and constantly make an effort to find and embrace new challenges and responsibilities. I pay great attention to detail, as exemplified in my work in which I implement innovation and effectiveness at the highest standard of technical excellence that I can.

Outside of programming, I am an avid maker and love crafting and doing things with my own hands. I have a variety of interests from origami to classic car restoration which you can find on my blogs.

On this page you will find some links to access information about me and some of my side projects/hobbies. Feel free to look around!!
