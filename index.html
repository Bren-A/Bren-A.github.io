About Me

My name is Brendan and I am a programmer.

I graduated from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) with a Bachelors in Linguistics and Computer Science due to my interest in both languages and technology. I love learning, which is why I constantly make an effort to find and embrace new challenges and responsibilities (such as building this website). I pay great attention to detail, as exemplified in my work in which I implement innovation and effectiveness at the highest standard of technical excellence that I can.

I bring this work ethic with me to every scenario that I encounter. Outside of programming, I am an avid maker and love crafting and doing things with my own hands. I have a variety of interests from origami to classic car restoration.
